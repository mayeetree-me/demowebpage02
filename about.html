Our Legacy & Philosophy

Bridging the Intellectual Gap between Medicine and Humanity.

SAMPRITI was founded on the belief that a physician's greatest tool is not just a stethoscope, but a profound understanding of the human narrative. We curate the intersection of clinical excellence and creative expression.

"Art is the heartbeat of medicine, and medicine is the canvas of our humanity."

The Foundation

Established in late 2014 by a group of visionary medical students, SAMPRITI emerged as a sanctuary for those who found comfort in both pathology and poetry.

What started as a modest newsletter has evolved into a nationally recognized Medical Society, fostering a community of over 5,000 clinicians and students.

The SAMPRITI Motto
"Symphony of Science, Empathy in Practice."

Our Mission

To nurture the 'Complete Physician' by providing a platform for literary, artistic, and clinical excellence through peer-reviewed journals and collaborative workshops.

The Vision

To be the global standard for medical-humanities integration, ensuring clinical precision is always balanced by humanistic depth.

Where Science
Meets the Canvas.

Bridging Science and Artistry through rigorous medical discourse and creative medical illustration.

"Medicine is the most beautiful of all arts."

SAMPRITI was founded on the belief that scientific discovery and artistic expression are not mutually exclusive. As a premier medical journal society, we curate research that challenges the intellect and illustrations that captivate the eye. Our mission is to provide a platform for medical professionals to share their clinical findings while celebrating the aesthetic essence of biological life.
